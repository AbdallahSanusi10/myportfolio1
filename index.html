Web Developer!

What sets me apart is the unique perspective I bring from my background in chemical engineering. Collaboration is a key value for me, and I thrive in team environments that foster creativity and innovation. I am a dedicated learner, constantly adapting to new technologies and frameworks, and actively seeking opportunities to collaborate and grow with fellow developers.

I invite you to explore my portfolio, where you can see my web development projects and experience firsthand the intuitive and visually captivating websites I have created.
